What Really Matters

True Accuracy (Beyond Display Resolution)

Many scales show “0.1 g” on the screen, but that's only readability—the smallest step displayed. It doesn't guarantee actual measurement accuracy. Without published error tolerances, readings can vary more than expected, especially under real conditions. Acaia scales specify performance (e.g., repeatability of 0.1 g, linearity within 1 d or ±0.2 g on models like Lunar and Pearl) across the full range, even with temperature changes or vibration. We test and publish these specs clearly so you know what to expect. Scales without defined tolerances are usually consumer-grade.

Environment-Resistant Design

Ordinary scales often drift with heat, cold, humidity, or vibrations—common in cafés. Acaia scales are engineered to resist these effects (including individual testing with hot water to minimize drift), delivering stable, repeatable readings shot after shot.

Built for Long, Demanding Use

Café scales operate for hours with constant taring, pouring, and temperature fluctuations. Our industrial foundation ensures Acaia scales hold reliability over extended, heavy use—unlike basic scales not designed for continuous precision.
